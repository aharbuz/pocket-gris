Based on what I know (up to May 2025), Apple Intelligence doesn't have a dedicated video background removal feature. Here's what exists in the Apple ecosystem:

**What Apple offers:**
- **Photos app** can lift subjects from **still images** (iOS 16+/macOS Ventura+) — tap and hold a subject to separate it from the background
- **iOS 17+** extended this to lift subjects from **paused video frames**, but it produces a still image, not a processed video
- **FaceTime** has a "Portrait" mode that blurs backgrounds in real-time, but that's only for video calls

**What it doesn't do:**
- There's no built-in tool to remove/replace backgrounds from recorded video clips across the OS

**Better alternatives for video background removal:**
- **Final Cut Pro** — has background removal/chroma key tools
- **DaVinci Resolve** (free) — solid keying and masking
- **RunwayML** — AI-powered video background removal in the browser
- **CapCut** — has auto background removal for video on mobile
